# TransNet

- 标题: TransNet: Transferable Neural Networks for Partial Differential Equations
- 发表: [ArXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2301.11701v1)
- 作者: Zezhong Zhang | Feng Bao | Lili Ju | Guannan Zhang

## Abstract·摘要

<table><tr><td width="50%">

Transfer learning for partial differential equations (PDEs) is to develop a pre-trained neural network that can be used to solve a wide class of PDEs.
Existing transfer learning approaches require much information of the target PDEs such as its formulation and/or data of its solution for pre-training.
In this work, we propose to construct transferable neural feature spaces from purely function approximation perspectives without using PDE information.
The construction of the feature space involves re-parameterization of the hidden neurons and uses auxiliary functions to tune the resulting feature space.
Theoretical analysis shows the high quality of the produced feature space, i.e., uniformly distributed neurons.
Extensive numerical experiments verify the outstanding performance of our method, including significantly improved transferability, e.g., using the same feature space for various PDEs with different domains and boundary conditions, and the superior accuracy, e.g., several orders of magnitude smaller mean squared error than the state of the art methods.

</td><td>

偏微分方程（PDEs）的迁移学习旨在构建一个预训练的神经网络，使其能够求解一大类不同的偏微分方程。
现有的迁移学习方法在预训练阶段通常需要大量关于目标 PDE 的信息，例如其具体控制方程形式和/或其解的数据。

本文提出一种不依赖任何 PDE 信息、仅从纯函数逼近角度出发构建可迁移神经特征空间的新方法。
该特征空间的构建通过对隐藏神经元进行重新参数化，并引入辅助函数对所得特征空间进行调节优化。

理论分析表明，所构造的特征空间具有优异的性质，即神经元在输入空间中呈现均匀分布。
大量数值实验验证了本方法的卓越性能：
- 显著提升的迁移能力：同一特征空间可成功应用于多种不同区域、不同边界条件的 PDE 求解；
- 更高的求解精度：与当前最先进的方法相比，均方误差（MSE）降低了数个数量级。
